Privacy

How the house treats your information

What we collect. If you join the house through our newsletter — that's all we store. An email address and which tier you chose. Payments are handled securely by Stripe.

What we do with it. We send you letters when the moon shifts. Recipes, rituals, playlists, prompts. No selling. No sharing. No third-party marketing. No spam. Ever.

Where it's stored. Your email is stored securely through Ghost, our newsletter platform. We do not use cookies or tracking scripts on this site.

Your rights. You can unsubscribe any time through the link in every email, or reach out via @maisoncache. We'll remove everything, no questions asked.

Content advisory. Maison Caché contains rooms and experiences that explore themes of sensuality, emotional intensity, and ritual. Some areas of the house — including The Sanctum — are intended for adults (18+). By entering these spaces, you acknowledge this.

Who we are. Maison Caché is an independent creative project based in Australia.
