# Stage 4 (part 1) — deepen the open rooms · ready to publish

Two rooms got deeper. **These versions replace the ones from the missing-rooms fix** — upload
these instead; they include that fix *and* the upgrades.

## The Hearth — now "tonight's warmth"
`hearth.html` is rebuilt. Instead of one recipe, the house now reads **tonight's real moon phase**
and hands you a small bundle: **eat this · drink this · play this · tend this corner**, plus a
small magick. Each phase has its own poem ("rest now, wolf" at the waning crescent). The
**"which sky are you under?"** toggle computes the season from the actual month — so the *"Autumn
in winter"* bug is gone for good. Gentle funnel: one bundle free → the full moon-cycle in the Grimoire.

- **The bundle content is DRAFT** — in your voice, but written by me as placeholder. Every dish,
  drink, song-cue, tending task and magick is yours to make real. Tell me and I'll swap any of them.
- The old recipe Hearth (the mushrooms, the spring lamb) is **backed up** as
  `hearth-OLD-recipes-backup.html`. Those full recipes are meant to live in the **Grimoire** now
  (the free site shows tonight's bundle; the full cycle is the paid draw). If you'd rather keep a
  free recipe or two on the site too, say the word and I'll fold them back in.

## The Perfume Atelier — door-out sharpened
The end-of-reading CTA now opens explicitly into **"scent & self"** — *what your scent says about
you, and how to use it* — and the button reads **"Learn your scent & self."**
