Privacy

How the house treats your information

What we collect. If you share your email — through our newsletter — that's all we store. An email, a date, which part of the house you were visiting.

What we do with it. We send you letters when the moon shifts. Recipes, rituals, playlists, prompts. No selling. No sharing. No spam. Ever.

Storage & payments. Your email is stored securely through Ghost, our newsletter platform. Payments are handled by Stripe.

Your rights. You can unsubscribe any time through the link in every email. Ask us to delete your data via @maisoncache. We'll remove it, no questions asked.

Content advisory. Maison Caché contains rooms that explore sensuality, emotional intensity, and ritual. The Sanctum is 18+ only.

Who we are. Maison Caché is an independent creative project based in Australia.
