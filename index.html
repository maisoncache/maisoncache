Privacy

How the house treats your information

What we collect. If you share your email — through Remember Me, The Sanctum, or the Archive — that's all we store. An email, a date, which part of the house you were visiting.

What we do with it. We use your email only to notify you when new rooms open. No selling. No sharing. No spam. Ever.

Your rights. Ask us to delete your email any time via @maisoncache. We'll remove it, no questions asked.

Content advisory. Maison Caché contains rooms that explore sensuality, emotional intensity, and ritual. The Sanctum is 18+ only.
